Route to Alpine Chalet

From Chur to Malix
  • Drive toward Lenzerheide
  • In Malix, turn right at the petrol station (follow signs to Brambrüesch)
Winter Access Info
  • Vehicle access is possible up to the ski lift car park
  • From 08:30–17:00, the road from the ski lift to the chalet is closed for sledging
  • Outside these hours, you can drive up to the chalet and park at Parking 1 or 2(6-minute walk if parked lower)
Parking Details
  • If parked at the chalet, car cannot be moved during 08:30–17:00
  • A parking card is provided at the chalet
  • Place the card clearly visible behind the windscreen
