Keep it Clean – Waste & Recycling

Use the Blue Bags

You’ll find official blue rubbish bags in the kitchen – please use these for your general waste.

Where to Dispose

When full, place the bags in the wooden barn opposite the main entrance.

Cardboard & Paper

Please separate cardboard and paper and place them in the same barn — we’ll take care of the rest!
