Chalet Guide

Heating & fireplace

How to adjust underfloor heating, light the stove (with safety tips)

  • Set the temperature using underfloor heating controls
  • Open the stove door and add kindling and logs
  • Ignite the match and close the door securely
Entertainment System

TV, Chromecast, Spotify/Netflix Login Instructions

  • Turn on the TV and select HDMI 1 input.
  • Use the remote to navigate Chromecast.
  • Login with your Spotify and Netflix Credentials
Kitchen & coffee machine

Kitchen appliances and coffee setup instructions

  • Use the oven dial to set heat and function.
  • Add a tab and start the dishwasher.
  • Fill water tank and press brew on the coffee machine.
Washer & dryer

Washing and drying instructions

  • Washer and dryer are in the bathroom closet.
  • Add detergent, select a program, and press start.
  • For drying, clean lint filter and select the dry mode.
