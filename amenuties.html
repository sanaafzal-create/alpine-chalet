Amenities

Free Parking

The parking spot number 1 or 2 is exclusively reserved for you during your stay.

Fully equipped kitchen

Our fully equipped kitchen includes a fondue and raclette set, wine glasses, and all essential cookware and utensils for preparing your meals..

Towels and bath shampoo / soap

We provide fresh towels, bath towels and shampoo/soap for your stay.

Baby bed and highchair

We provide a baby bed and highchair for our little guests upon request. Please let us know in advance if you need these items during your stay.

WLAN

WLAN is available at our building. Password is on printout inside the chalet.

Heating

The chalet features a traditional Swedish stove and radiators installed in all rooms. For added comfort, the bathroom is equipped with a towel rail that has integrated heating functionality.

Washing machine and tumble dryer

A washing machine and tumble dryer are available for your convenience. These appliances are free to use during your stay.
