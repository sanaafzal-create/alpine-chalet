Getting your Key

1

Locate your key box

You’ll find the key box mounted to the left of the entrance door.

2

Enter Your PIN

We’ll send you the 4-digit PIN code via email or SMS shortly before your arrival.

3

Unlock and Enjoy

Open the box, grab your key — you're all set to check in!
